# AlphaStar: Grandmaster Level in StarCraft II

*paper, 2019. Vinyals et al.*

Grandmaster-rank StarCraft II via imitation pretraining + off-policy actor-critic RL + league training (main + main-exploiter + league-exploiter agents). League play patches strategic weaknesses and avoids the cyclic forgetting of plain self-play. RSPO's StarCraft II evaluation domain.
