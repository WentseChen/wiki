# Spectral Geometry of LoRA Adapters Encodes Training Objective and Predicts Harmful Compliance

*paper, 2026. Roi Paul.*

Shows that per-layer spectral statistics of LoRA weight deltas (effective rank, stable rank, singular-value entropy) perfectly classify training objectives within a method family and predict harmful behavioral compliance with near-perfect dose-response correlation. Establishes spectral geometry as a viable LoRA adapter safety monitoring signal, while exposing cross-method generalization failure.
