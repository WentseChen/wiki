# LoRA Training Provably Converges to a Low-Rank Global Minimum or It Fails Loudly

*paper, 2025. Junsu Kim, Jaeyeon Kim, Ernest K. Ryu.*

Formal analysis of LoRA's loss landscape without linearization: training either reaches a low-rank global minimum or a high-rank spurious local minimum. Zero-initialization and weight decay induce an implicit bias toward the low-rank basin, explaining why rank collapse (not rank explosion) is the dominant outcome in practice.
