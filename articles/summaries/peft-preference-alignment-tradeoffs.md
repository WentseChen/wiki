# A Deep Dive into the Trade-Offs of Parameter-Efficient Preference Alignment Techniques

*paper, 2024. Thakkar et al. ACL 2024 (Main).*

300+ experiments across LoRA/QLoRA, SFT/DPO, 4 model families, and 2 alignment datasets reveal non-trivial trade-offs in PEFT-based preference alignment. Key finding: SFT sometimes outperforms DPO, and aligning to a distinct preference can boost downstream task performance. The most comprehensive LoRA-alignment trade-off analysis to date.
