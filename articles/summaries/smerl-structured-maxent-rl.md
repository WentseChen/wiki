# SMERL: One Solution is Not All You Need

*paper, 2020. Kumar, Kumar, Levine, Finn.*

Trains a latent-conditioned policy to find multiple near-optimal solutions to one MDP via a structured max-ent diversity bonus, gated by a near-optimality threshold. Yields few-shot extrapolation to test-time perturbations without training on them. Closest algorithmic sibling to RSPO.
