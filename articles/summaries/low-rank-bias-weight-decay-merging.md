# Low-rank bias, weight decay, and model merging in neural networks

*paper, 2025. Ilja Kuzborskij, Yasin Abbasi Yadkori.*

Formally proves that L2 weight decay produces parameter-gradient alignment, cross-layer norm preservation, and low-rank bias at stationary points of GD/GF, for shallow ReLU and deep linear networks. Also shows this low-rank structure enables model merging via weight summation under orthogonal input distributions. Provides the general theoretical foundation for why weight decay drives rank collapse in fine-tuning.
