# Rank-1 Approximation of Inverse Fisher for Natural Policy Gradients in Deep Reinforcement Learning

*paper, 2026. Yingxiao Huo, Satya Prakash Dash, Radu Stoican, Samuel Kaski, Mingfei Sun.*

Proposes rank-1 approximation of the inverse Fisher Information Matrix for scalable natural policy gradients; proves faster convergence than vanilla PG under stated conditions. The FIM-rank angle is directly relevant to rank collapse: sparse/high-variance RL rewards produce a degenerate (low-rank) policy FIM, formally grounding why reward sparsity induces low-rank parameter updates.
