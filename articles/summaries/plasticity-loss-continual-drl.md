# Loss of Plasticity in Continual Deep Reinforcement Learning

*paper, 2023. Abbas, Zhao, Modayil, White, Machado.*

Builds a multi-game continual Atari benchmark and shows clear plasticity loss across billions of frames; attributes it to weight-norm growth and dying ReLUs. Replacing ReLU with Concatenated ReLU (CReLU) drastically reduces dead units and restores plasticity — an architectural complement to continual backprop.
