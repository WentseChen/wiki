# LoRA Learns Less and Forgets Less

*paper, 2024. Biderman et al. TMLR (Featured).*

Canonical empirical comparison showing LoRA substantially underperforms full fine-tuning on in-domain tasks but better preserves base model OOD performance. Full fine-tuning learns weight updates with effective rank 10-100x greater than typical LoRA rank — likely the root cause of the gap. Establishes the "learns less, forgets less" framing for LoRA behavior.
