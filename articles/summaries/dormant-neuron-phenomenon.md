# The Dormant Neuron Phenomenon in Deep Reinforcement Learning

*paper, 2023. Sokar, Agarwal, Castro, Evci.*

Quantifies a "dormancy" score for hidden units and shows the dormant fraction grows monotonically in DQN/Rainbow training. ReDo periodically reinitializes dormant units (incoming + outgoing weights), recovering capacity and improving Atari and DM-Control across replay ratios — the closest mechanistic cousin of continual backprop on the RL side.
