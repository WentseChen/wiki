# A Generalized Training Approach for Multiagent Learning

*paper, 2020. Muller, Omidshafiei, Rowland, Tuyls, Perolat, Liu, Hennes, Marris, Lanctot, Hughes, Wang, Lever, Heess, Graepel, Munos.*

α-PSRO: replaces PSRO's Nash meta-solver with α-Rank, extending PSRO to general-sum, many-player games and removing equilibrium-selection ambiguity. Faster convergence than Nash-based PSRO on 3-5-player Kuhn / Leduc poker.
