# Small Singular Values Matter: A Random Matrix Analysis of Transformer Models

*paper, 2024. Max Staats, Matthias Thamm, Bernd Rosenow.*

Uses the Marchenko-Pastur RMT law as a zero-information baseline to identify learned structure in transformer singular-value spectra: deviations appear at both ends, not just the top. Small SVs carry learned structure (aligned with activation covariance eigenvectors) and become the third most influential component after fine-tuning. Provides a principled spectral framework for detecting rank collapse during RL fine-tuning.
