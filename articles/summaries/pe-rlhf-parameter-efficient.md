# Parameter Efficient Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback

*paper, 2024. Sidahmed et al.*

Large-scale empirical study showing LoRA-based PE-RLHF matches full RLHF performance across 6 diverse tasks while delivering 90% faster reward modeling, 30% faster RL training, and up to 50% memory savings. Covers both reward model and policy fine-tuning phases, making it the most comprehensive LoRA+RLHF efficiency evaluation to date.
