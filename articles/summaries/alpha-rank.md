# α-Rank: Multi-Agent Evaluation by Evolution

*paper, 2019. Omidshafiei, Papadimitriou, Piliouras, Tuyls, Rowland, Lespiau, Czarnecki, Lanctot, Perolat, Munos.*

Evolutionary-dynamics ranking concept (Markov-Conley chains) that scales to many-player general-sum empirical games and runs in polynomial time. Unique solution, no Nash-selection ambiguity — directly usable as a PSRO meta-solver. Validated on AlphaGo, AlphaZero, MuJoCo Soccer, Poker.
