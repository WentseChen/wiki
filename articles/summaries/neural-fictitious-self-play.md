# Deep Reinforcement Learning from Self-Play in Imperfect-Information Games

*paper, 2016. Heinrich, Silver.*

NFSP: first end-to-end deep-RL Nash solver for imperfect-info games. Q-network learns best responses, supervised policy network learns the average strategy. Converges in Leduc poker (where independent Q-learning diverges), competitive with expert-engineered programs on limit Texas Hold'em. Direct predecessor and standard baseline for PSRO.
