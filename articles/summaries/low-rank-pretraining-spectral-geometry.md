# Beyond Perplexity: A Geometric and Spectral Study of Low-Rank Pre-Training

*paper, 2026. Shivagunde et al.*

Shows that low-rank pre-training methods (GaLore, Fira, CoLA, SLTrain, ReLoRA) converge to geometrically distinct solutions from full-rank training — even when perplexity is matched — using 16 metrics spanning loss landscape geometry, spectral structure, and activation similarity. Establishes that matched perplexity does not imply matched weight geometry; spectral probes reveal real capacity differences.
