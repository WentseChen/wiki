# Effective Diversity in Population Based RL (DvD)

*paper, 2020. Parker-Holder, Pacchiano, Choromanski, Roberts.*

Population diversity = determinant of kernel over learned behavioral embeddings — the volume the population spans, not pairwise distances. Online-adapts the diversity coefficient, with ES and policy-gradient variants. Strong baseline for population-based diversity in RL.
