# Continuously Discovering Novel Strategies via Reward-Switching Policy Optimization

*paper, 2022. Zihan Zhou, Wei Fu, Bingliang Zhang, Yi Wu.*

RSPO iteratively discovers diverse RL policies by switching between extrinsic task reward and an intrinsic diversity reward based on trajectory-level novelty vs. previously found policies. The trajectory-based novelty signal steers exploration toward undiscovered local optima, recovering qualitatively different strategies across particle worlds, MuJoCo, multi-agent games, and StarCraft II.
