# Arbitrary Entropy Policy Optimization Breaks The Exploration Bottleneck of Reinforcement Learning

*paper, 2025. Wang et al.*

Names entropy collapse in GRPO as the exploration bottleneck of LLM RL fine-tuning. Reformulates entropy regularization as a policy-gradient problem via REINFORCE on temperature-adjusted samples, avoiding the reward-vs-entropy trade-off of MaxEnt baselines. Dual of weight-side rank collapse: policy-level entropy collapse and parameter-level rank collapse are two faces of the same low-rank-update pathology.
