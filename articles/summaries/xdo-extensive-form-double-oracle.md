# XDO: A Double Oracle Algorithm for Extensive-Form Games

*paper, 2021. McAleer, Lanier, Wang, Baldi, Fox.*

PSRO mixes at the root and can need exponentially many iterations on sequential games; XDO mixes at every infostate of the extensive form, guaranteeing linear convergence. Neural XDO is the first deep-RL Nash solver for high-dimensional continuous-action sequential games.
