# DADS: Dynamics-Aware Skill Discovery

*paper, 2020. Sharma, Gu, Levine, Kumar, Hausman.*

Unsupervised skill discovery that jointly learns a continuous latent skill space and skill-conditioned dynamics models, making skills both diverse and predictable. Predictability enables MPC planning in latent skill space for zero-shot downstream transfer.
