# Overcoming Catastrophic Forgetting in Neural Networks (EWC)

*paper, 2017. Kirkpatrick et al.*

Canonical regularization-based continual-learning method: anchor each task's converged weights with a quadratic penalty scaled by diagonal Fisher information. Demonstrated on permuted MNIST and sequential Atari. Represents the opposite axis of the plasticity/forgetting trade-off the Dohare paper sharpens.
