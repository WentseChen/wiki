# LoRA vs Full Fine-tuning: An Illusion of Equivalence

*paper, 2024. Shuttleworth, Andreas, Torralba, Sharma (MIT).*

Spectral analysis reveals that LoRA creates "intruder dimensions" — anomalous high-rank singular vectors absent in full fine-tuning — which causally drive catastrophic forgetting and accumulate destructively in continual learning. Directly challenges the assumption of LoRA/full-FT equivalence and provides a post-hoc mitigation (scaling down intruder singular values).
