# Multi-Agent Training beyond Zero-Sum with Correlated Equilibrium Meta-Solvers

*paper, 2021. Marris, Muller, Lanctot, Tuyls, Graepel.*

JPSRO extends PSRO to n-player general-sum games using (coarse) correlated equilibria as the meta-solver. Introduces Maximum Gini Correlated Equilibrium (MGCE) for principled selection from the CE polytope, with convergence guarantees and higher social welfare than Nash.
