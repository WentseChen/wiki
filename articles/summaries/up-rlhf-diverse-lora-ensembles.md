# Uncertainty-Penalized Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback with Diverse Reward LoRA Ensembles

*paper, 2023. Zhai et al.*

UP-RLHF uses diverse LoRA ensembles (diversified via nuclear norm maximization) to quantify reward model uncertainty, then penalizes uncertain policy updates to mitigate RLHF overoptimization. Notable for explicitly applying rank-maximization to LoRA ensemble design — a counterpoint to rank-collapse concerns.
