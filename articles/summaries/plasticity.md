# Loss of plasticity in deep continual learning

*paper, 2024. Dohare, Hernandez-Garcia, Lan, Rahman, Mahmood, Sutton.*

Shows that standard deep nets gradually lose the ability to learn under continual / non-stationary training — across supervised and RL benchmarks, optimizers and architectures — and proposes **continual backpropagation**, which reinitializes a small fraction of least-used units each step to keep plasticity indefinitely.
