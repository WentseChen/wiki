# How Much is Too Much? Exploring LoRA Rank Trade-offs for Retaining Knowledge and Domain Robustness

*paper, 2025. Rathore et al. AACL-IJCNLP 2025.*

Rank sweep study showing LoRA rank has a non-monotonic effect on performance: optimal ranks match or exceed full SFT, but increasing rank past a threshold accelerates forgetting without in-domain gains. Spectral and attention analysis reveals structural representation shifts correlated with OOD robustness degradation.
