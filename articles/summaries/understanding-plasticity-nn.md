# Understanding Plasticity in Neural Networks

*paper, 2023. Lyle, Zheng, Nikishin, Avila Pires, Pascanu, Dabney.*

Disentangles proposed causes of plasticity loss and argues the dominant driver is loss-landscape curvature (Hessian eigenvalue blowup), not dead/saturated units — which are neither necessary nor sufficient. Layer norm, careful init, and optimizer choice preserve plasticity without explicit resets.
