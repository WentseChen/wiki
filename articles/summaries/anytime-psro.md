# Anytime PSRO for Two-Player Zero-Sum Games

*paper, 2022. McAleer, Wang, Lanier, Lanctot, Baldi, Sandholm, Fox.*

ADO/APSRO fix PSRO's non-monotone exploitability by picking the meta-distribution that minimizes exploitability against any policy in the full game — not just the restricted strategy set. Guarantees monotone (anytime) exploitability decrease and Nash convergence.
