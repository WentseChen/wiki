# Diverse Nearly Optimal Policies with Successor Features

*paper, 2021. Zahavy, O'Donoghue, Barreto, Mnih, Flennerhag, Singh.*

CMDP framing: maximize a diversity reward (minimize SF correlation across policies) subject to a near-optimality constraint on task reward. Identifies why discrimination-based diversity (DIAYN-style) can collapse to suboptimality when forced near-optimal. Different gaits on DM Control.
