# A Shared Low-Rank Adaptation Approach to Personalized RLHF

*paper, 2025. Liu et al. AISTATS 2025.*

Applies LoRA in the aggregated parameter space of personalized reward functions to handle heterogeneous human preferences in RLHF, exploiting shared low-rank structure across users. Provides sample complexity guarantees and demonstrates effectiveness on real-world datasets. Bridges LoRA theory with personalized alignment.
