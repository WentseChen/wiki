# Maintaining Plasticity in Continual Learning via Regenerative Regularization (L2 Init)

*paper, 2023. Kumar, Marklund, Van Roy.*

Proposes L2 Init: replace standard L2 decay with a penalty pulling parameters toward their *initial* values. One-line change, beats EWC, MAS, shrink-and-perturb, and continual backprop on Dohare-style continual supervised benchmarks. The cleanest "regularize, don't reset" alternative.
