# Measuring the Intrinsic Dimension of Objective Landscapes

*paper, 2018. Chunyuan Li, Heerad Farkhoor, Rosanne Liu, Jason Yosinski. ICLR 2018.*

Seminal paper defining the intrinsic dimension of objective landscapes: the minimum random-subspace dimensionality in which optimization finds a solution. Shows that RL and supervised learning problems have far smaller intrinsic dimensions than their parameter counts, provides cross-domain difficulty comparisons, and motivates LoRA/PEFT by establishing that fine-tuning solutions live in a low-dimensional subspace.
