# A Unified Game-Theoretic Approach to Multiagent Reinforcement Learning

*paper, 2017. Lanctot, Zambaldi, Gruslys, Lazaridou, Tuyls, Perolat, Silver, Graepel.*

Introduces PSRO (Policy Space Response Oracles), unifying deep RL best-response training with empirical game-theoretic meta-strategy selection. Shows independent RL overfits to its training opponents, generalizes fictitious play / double oracle, and quantifies the effect via joint-policy correlation on gridworld coordination and Leduc poker.
