# MAP-Elites

*paper, 2015. Mouret & Clune.*

Quality-diversity algorithm: discretize a behavior space into cells, keep the best solution per cell. Output is a diverse high-performing archive rather than one optimum, illuminating how performance varies across behavior dimensions. Backbone of modern QD.
