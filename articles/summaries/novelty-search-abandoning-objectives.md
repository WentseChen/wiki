# Abandoning Objectives: Novelty Search

*paper, 2011. Lehman & Stanley.*

Drop the fitness function entirely; reward only behavioral novelty against an archive. On deceptive problems (maze nav, biped) novelty alone beats objective-based search. Foundational to QD, MAP-Elites, and trajectory-novelty rewards used in RSPO.
