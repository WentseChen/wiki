# Joint PSRO (JPSRO)

PSRO extension to n-player general-sum extensive-form games that uses (coarse) correlated equilibrium solvers in place of Nash. Pairs with the Maximum Gini Correlated Equilibrium (MGCE) selection rule and is guaranteed to converge to a (C)CE of the underlying game.
