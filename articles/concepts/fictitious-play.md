# Fictitious Play

Classic game-theoretic learning dynamic: at each round, every player best-responds to the empirical average of opponents' past play. Converges to Nash in two-player zero-sum and other restricted classes. PSRO generalizes fictitious play by replacing the empirical-average meta-strategy with a Nash (or other) solution of an empirical game.
