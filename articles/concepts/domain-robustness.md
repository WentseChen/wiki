# Domain Robustness

A model's ability to maintain performance on out-of-distribution or held-out domains after fine-tuning on a target domain. LoRA tends to preserve domain robustness better than full fine-tuning at the cost of lower in-domain peak performance; higher LoRA ranks can erode this advantage.
