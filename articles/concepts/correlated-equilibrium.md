# Correlated Equilibrium (CE)

Generalization of Nash equilibrium where a public signal recommends strategies to each player and no player can gain by unilaterally deviating from the recommendation. Tractable to compute (LP) in n-player general-sum games, forms a convex polytope, and admits cooperative/coordination solutions with higher social welfare than Nash.
