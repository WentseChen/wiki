# Neural Fictitious Self-Play (NFSP)

End-to-end deep-RL approximation of fictitious play for imperfect-information games. A Q-network learns best responses against the opponent's average strategy while a separate policy network learns the player's own average strategy via supervised learning over its action history. First scalable Nash solver for imperfect-info games without handcrafted abstractions.
