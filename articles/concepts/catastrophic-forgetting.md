# catastrophic forgetting

Sharp loss of performance on previously-learned tasks when a network is trained sequentially on new tasks. The classical pathology of continual learning — distinct from, and often *opposite* to, [[plasticity-loss]] (one preserves old knowledge, the other preserves the ability to learn new).
