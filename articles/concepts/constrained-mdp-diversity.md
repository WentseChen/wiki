# Constrained-MDP framing of diverse RL

Formulates diverse policy discovery as a CMDP: the objective is a diversity reward (e.g. inter-policy SF correlation or state-occupancy distance), and task performance enters as a hard near-optimality constraint rather than a weighted bonus. Decouples the diversity-vs-quality trade-off into a single interpretable threshold.
