# Anytime Double Oracle (ADO / APSRO)

Variant of double oracle for two-player zero-sum games where the meta-distribution is chosen to minimize exploitability against any policy in the *full* unrestricted game, not just the restricted strategy set. Guarantees monotone (anytime) exploitability decrease and Nash convergence. APSRO is the deep-RL realization.
