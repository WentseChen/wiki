# A Practitioner's Guide to Multi-turn Agentic Reinforcement Learning

**Link:** https://openreview.net/forum?id=K6T0o875zF

## Summary
Systematically ablates the design space of multi-turn agentic RL across three pillars — environment, reward, policy — on TextWorld, ALFWorld, and SWE-gym. Distills findings into a practical training recipe; key takeaway: dense turn-rewards help but stability hinges on the RL algorithm.

## Key points
- 3-pillar design space: environment / reward / policy
- Simple-env signals predict harder-env generalization
- Dense turn rewards × RL algo choice interact strongly
- PPO vs RLOO trade-offs characterized
- SFT-vs-RL ratio under fixed compute
